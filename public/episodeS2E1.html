Case Summary

Detective Jake Peralta is assigned to a dangerous undercover operation involving the mafia, forcing him to abandon his usual carefree behavior.

This episode explores trust, risk, and sacrifice while showing the squad adapting to higher-stakes police work beyond their precinct.
